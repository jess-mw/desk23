Behind this box of text lies a globe that is waiting to be explored

Scattered on the globe are bar charts of various colours

Each bar chart represents the literacy rate of a country

Click on a bar chart and a tiny box with information regarding the country's literacy rate will pop up

The different colours of the bar charts represent how high or low the literacy rate is

The lower the literacy rate, the redder the bar chart will be

The higher the literacy rate, the yellower the bar chart will be

Have fun exploring!

Spin the globe in any direction by clicking and dragging

Click on a bar-chart of a country to view its literacy rates and other data

Use two fingers to zoom in or out

Drag the slider left or right to change which year we are currently in

Try using your keys to simulate the bar graphs changing
